Olive, UX Design

This project was in response to the brief set by Career Foundry.

Strategic use of colour

The colour green encourages emotions of ambition, growth and amazement whilst orange sparks emotions of anticipation, interest and joy. This combination results in the human feeling of hope. Olive is designed to keep users motivated on their health and wellbeing goals - hope is key for the continuation of use, ensuring the user can rely on consistent tones of inspiration and trust.

Rewarding interactions

Keeping motivation high is a key objective for Olive. Illustrations and animations will be used when users are making good progress and when they have finished a task. These will be present throughout the app. Future iterations will consider the use of a mascot and the use of gender neutral charectaristics.

Development of personality

For the copy and tone of voice - the tone will remain light and friendly, whilst also motivational. Using the user’s name to remain personable and reinforce the message that the app is tailored to the user’s needs. Timely motivational notifications will be present on the user’s phone and will pop up whilst they are using the app - keeping these short, enthusiastic and straight to the point will be key in not overwhelming the user.
